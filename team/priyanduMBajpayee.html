Priyandu M Bajpayee is a Senior Research Fellow and PhD Scholar in Economics at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. His research examines the intersections of energy poverty, healthcare access, and development economics, with a focus on advancing inclusive policies that enhance wellbeing. A gold medalist during his masters in Economics from the Central University of South Bihar, he has published in leading international journals and contributes actively to academic teaching and global conferences.
