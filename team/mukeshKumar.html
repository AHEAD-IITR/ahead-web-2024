Mukesh Kumar is a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences, Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. His work lies at the intersection of development economics, public health, and gender issues. Under the broader theme of Nutrition Transition, his research examines the issues of maternal and child (mal)nutrition, long-run human capital and well-being implications of childhood health, and program utilisation in India and other developing countries.
