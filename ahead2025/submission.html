Submission Instructions

Instructions for Submitting a Paper or Poster

  1. Single Submission Rule: Only one submission as presenting author is allowed in the Call for Papers for AHEAD 2025. Abstract submitters may be listed as co-authors on other submissions.
  2. Submission Types: Authors should submit either a short abstract (200 words), an extended abstract (2-4 pages, including tables), or a full paper describing the topic, theoretical focus, data, research methods, and expected findings. The extended abstract must be uploaded to the conference submission website. Preference will be given to full paper submissions.
  3. Author Information: The submitting author should provide the full names, affiliations, and email addresses of all co-authors and indicate which author will present the paper at the conference. This information can be changed later.
  4. Submission Categories: Abstracts should be submitted to either a theme topic or a session topic. If a paper is not included in a member-organized session, indicate a relevant theme for consideration in a theme session.
  5. Poster Sessions: Authors who only want to present a poster should submit to a theme and select “poster session.” Papers not selected for oral sessions will be considered for poster sessions.
  6. Review Process: All submissions will be reviewed by two anonymous reviewers before consideration by session organizers or theme conveners.
  7. Presentation Limits: Only one appearance as a presenting author of a paper or poster is allowed. Co-author roles, session chair, or discussant roles do not count toward this limit.
