Submission Instructions

Instructions for Submitting a Paper or Poster

  1. Only one submission as presenting author will be allowed in the Call for Papers for AHEAD 2025 conference. Abstract submitters may, however, be listed as co-authors on other submissions.
  2. Authors should submit either a short abstract (200 words), an extended abstract (2-4 pages, including tables), or a full paper (5000 â€“ 8000 words) describing the topic, theoretical focus, data, research methods, and expected findings. Preference will be given to full paper submissions.
  3. The submitting author should provide the full name, affiliations, and email addresses of all co-authors listed on the submission and indicate which author will most likely present the paper at the AHEAD 2025 conference. This information can be changed later.
  4. Abstracts should be submitted to either a theme topic or a session topic. Those submitting a paper to a session topic can indicate a theme that is most relevant to the paper in case the paper is not included in the member-organized session so that it can be considered for inclusion in a theme session. These are specified on the AHEAD 2025 conference tracks.
  5. Poster sessions: Authors who only want to present a poster should submit to a theme and select "poster session." Papers submitted to a theme of that are not selected for a regular oral session may be considered for a poster session of AHEAD 2025 conference.
  6. All submissions will be reviewed by 2 anonymous reviewers.
  7. A presenter can also serve as a session chair or discussant.
